What are Tachyons?

Tachyons are hypothetical particles that travel faster than light. According to special relativity, they have imaginary mass and cannot slow down to the speed of light.

Scientific Theories

Albert Einstein's Theory of relativity suggests that particles with real mass cannot exceed the speed of light. However, some quantum field theories propose the existence of tachyons.

MY Theories

Tachyons

If we can go faster than the speed of light, then we can go to the past; we can go to the future by gravity. Hence we can time travel.

Tachyons in Sci-Fi

Tachyons have been used in sci-fi stories for time travel, faster than light communication, and space travel.
