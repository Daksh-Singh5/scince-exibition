What is a Periscope?

A periscope is an optical device used to see over or around obstacles using mirrors or prisms.

How it Works

Light reflects off mirrors placed at 45-degree angles, allowing the viewer to see objects that are out of direct sight.

Applications

Periscopes are used in submarines, tanks, and even in simple science experiments to understand reflection and optics.

Build Your Own Periscope

Use a cardboard box, two mirrors, and some tape to create a simple periscope for experiments!
